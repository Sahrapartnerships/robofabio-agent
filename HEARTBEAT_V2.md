## 🚨 INTELLIGENT ALERT SYSTEM

### 🟢 GREEN — All Good
**Condition:** All goals on track, no blockers >12h, no errors  
**Action:** Reply `HEARTBEAT_OK` only

### 🟡 YELLOW — Attention Needed
**Condition:** Blocker >12h, deadline approaching, opportunity spotted  
**Action:** Brief notification with context

### 🟠 ORANGE — Action Required
**Condition:** Blocker >24h, recurring error, goal at risk  
**Action:** Detailed alert with recommendation

### 🔴 RED — Critical
**Condition:** System failure, security issue, critical deadline missed  
**Action:** Immediate alert with escalation
